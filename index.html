Built Python · Claude API
AI Agent for Quarterly Earnings Tracking
Problem
Manual tracking of quarterly earnings required downloading research reports, writing notes, and drafting a distribution email to the team — taking ~45 minutes per company.
Solution
Using the Claude API and Python, I built an AI agent that generates earnings update notes and automatically prepares a team email with the notes, transcript, and relevant broker research attached.
Potential Impact
Reduced from ~45 minutes to under 1 minute per company — saving potentially ~20 hours per quarter and ~80 hours per year for me. Even more for organisations if used by all analysts.
